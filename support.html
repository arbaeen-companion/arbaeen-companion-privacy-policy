Support for Arbaeen Companion

Arbaeen Companion is a bilingual (English/Urdu) companion app for pilgrims undertaking the Arbaeen walk from Najaf to Karbala — offering ziyarat texts, dhikr and duas, safety guides, and offline-ready content for the road.

Contact

For questions, feedback, or issues with the app, please email: arbaeencompanion@gmail.com

Common Questions

Does the app work without internet?
Yes — all guides, ziyarat texts, and duas are available offline. The Ziyarat Arbaeen recitation audio can be downloaded in advance from the app for offline playback during the walk.

Does the app collect my personal data?
No. The app requires no account and collects no personal information. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

I found a bug or have a suggestion — what do I do?
Please email arbaeencompanion@gmail.com with a description of the issue and, if possible, your device model and the app version.
